Part Description Cost
cpu Central processing unit. Get a ryzen 3600. around 260 CAD
motherboard Connects other parts together. Get a B450. around 150 CAD for a decent one.
ram Your memory. 16 gb if you're a plebs like me. 32+ if you're a pro. Make sure you're getting ddr4. Forget about timing and speed. around 100 CAD/16 gb set.
psu The power supply. Don't cheap out. Take the TDP sum of your components with a grain of salt, especially if you're overclocking. However, for this build, I suggest a 500 watt semi modular model (SFX for some ITX builds. But that's another discussion for another day) around 90 CAD
case Choose a pretty one with okay airflow. Glass panels might look pretty, but they can also suffocate your components. A mesh-front helps with airflow. around 110 CAD
cpu cooler The stock cooler is fine. But for edginess points you can get a giant dual tower air cooler like the nh-d15 or an AIO liquid cooler. Don't even think about a custom loop for a Ryzen 3600. potentially 0. Or spend 1000 CAD for a pc-master-race worthy custom loop
gpu The graphics card. This is the real deal. You should allocate the biggest chunk of your budget for this bad boy. Prepare to spend at least 400 CAD just for a mid-tier card. 200 to 1600 CAD. Sweet spot for most people lies around 400.
case fans Bring air closer to your gpu and cpu. Also propels the hot air out. Install some at the back of your case as exhaust fans. And some at the front too if you have any left. 50 CAD for a set of 5 from Deepcool. That's not bad.
SSD Solid State Drive. Something to store your data in. Get an NVMe. Makes your life easier. Add 2.5 inch ones later on for additional capacity. Please don't get a hard drive. around 100 CAD for 512 Gb

Accessory

Accessories don't matter for dull boring people. But if you're the type looking to download ram, you should also consider adding lots of RGB to your build. There are several performance boosts you can attain from it. They vary by the color:

  • red: adds 10% overclocking potential without VRM issues. Additional 5% boost when used in combo with each AMD hardware used.
  • blue: 10% boost to cooling. Using Team Blue's chips gives no additional benefits.
  • green: 10% reduction in power draw. 10% boost to minimum fps when using Nvidia gpus. Also enable RTX capability to GTX cards once per turn. Shout "Nvidia fanboy" to activate.
