A Guide To Elevate Your Listening Experience

Headphones

This is the most important upgrade. Look for a headphone that has a balanced sound signature. Design can influence sound. Open-air heapdhones tend to have a bigger soundstage. Closed-backs have the upperhand with good punchy bass.

Dac

A DAC stands for "Digital Analog Converter". This device is what transforms your music from digital bits of information to an analog signal for your headphones. For a seemingly simple task, many DACs differ in quality. Look for a DAC with a high SNR (signal-to-noise ratio), and low distortion.

Amplifier

An amplifier gives energy and dynamic range to your headphones. Many people confuse this trait with volume. Good dynamic range will make your headphones have punchy bass, clarity, smoothness, and vibrancy in the sound it produces regardless of the volume you play your music.
